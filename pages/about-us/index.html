Company History

Established over a decade ago, our company has thrived in engineering services and manufacturing landscape. With a vertically integrated product testing and manufacturing facility spanning 50,000 sqft, we seamlessly blend on-shore and off-shore service delivery. Our dynamic team of over 50 experienced engineers has been the driving force behind our global presence, with operational hubs in India (Hyderabad) and the USA (Atlanta, GA).

FACILITY

Welcome to Alcom Facilities – a sprawling expanse spanning 150,000 square feet across multiple strategic locations. Our state-of-the-art facilities offer ample space for seamless production, warehousing, and logistics operations. Strategically positioned within 600 kilometers of the nearest seaport, our manufacturing unit ensures efficient import and export operations. At Alcom, we take pride in our onsite bonded warehouse, streamlining custom procedures and significantly reducing lead times. Explore a world of efficiency and reliability with Alcom Facilities.

EQUIPMENT

Alcom is dedicated to cutting-edge manufacturing, featuring a range of specialized equipment such as Customized DRILLING machines, PLASTMAK Machines, ALPHTECH Machines, MIRAGE units, DECALUM MACHINES, and high-performing MILLING MACHINES. Our facility also includes FINISHING MACHINES, DRYER Machines, WAXING units, LASER systems, CLIPPING machines, and advanced Packing Machines. Complemented by vital auxiliary machinery, like Air Compressors, Diesel Generator, and Multiple Mechanical Presses, our commitment to precision and efficiency is evident. We take pride in creating a manufacturing environment where state-of-the-art equipment meets expertise, ensuring each product is crafted with unparalleled precision and quality.
